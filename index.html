antonelmvik

Hey there, I'm Anton Elmvik - a musician and audio tech from Finland. I've spent my life plucking strings and creating sounds, as well as studying and working various jobs.

In 2022, I graduated with a B.A in music production from Dalarna University, but I also hold degrees in musicology from Åbo Akademi University and information technology from Turku Vocational Institute. You can read both of my theses here.

These days, I split my time between playing in various bands/projects and working at the Culture Department of Pargas.

Connect with me on Instagram, and feel free to drop me an email at antonelmvik@gmail.com.
